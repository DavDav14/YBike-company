YBIKE

YBIKE is a motorcycle dealer formed on December 30, 2016 and based in Jakarta,

Indonesia. YBIKE sells motorcycles from 2 famous brands of motorcycle: Vixian and

Kawasako. The dealer is led by Carvin Joestar. The products are kept inside a secure

warehouse and covered with a large fabric to keep them clean of dusts.

Carvin Joestar...

in his childhood Is a motorcycle maniac where since childhood he loved to ride motorcycles

to get around with his father who is also a motorcycle maniac. He loves to collect motorbikes

from Kawasaki and Vixian, which makes him want to create a dealership that sells both products,

Finally, together with his friends, he succeeded in establishing YBike Dealer to fulfill his dream

of owning his own motorcycle dealer

There are 2 legendary motorcycle products here, namely Vixian and Kawasako which you can get
